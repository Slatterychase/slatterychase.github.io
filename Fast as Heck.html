Fast as Heck - Personal Project

AI Engineer - Unity, C#

Fast as Heck is a roguelike rotation racer where you spin your phone to spin the world. Fast as Heck draws inspiration from Crazy Taxi, Diablo, and Blink-182 poster’s to deliver a no brakes neon injected shootathon. Grind demons into the asphalt and leave them in the dust as you race to deliver lost souls home. Fast as Heck combines the deep system-based gameplay of a roguelike with the approachable “pick up and play” gameplay of an infinite runner.

My work on this project included:

  • Implementing high level behaviors such as perception and attacking that all enemies use.
  • Effectively utilyzing the Unity Nav Mesh system in order to create behaviors and actions that simulate a physics based system, without requiring force application that could be effected by other physics objects in the scene.
  • Creating enemy specific behaviors, including a charging enemy, rangedattack enemy that keeps away from the player, and a laser enemy that acts like a tower, shooting a beam that follows the player.
  • Designing and implementing the system in a way that our designer could easily edit by utilizing the Unity Gizmo system to visualize high level behaviors such as attack and detection ranges.
